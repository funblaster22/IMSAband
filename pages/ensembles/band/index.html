A band is typically comprised of all the non-string instruments, like the flute, clarinet, oboe, saxophone, trumpet, trombone, tuba, and percussion. IMSA has two levels of band, Concert Band and Wind Ensemble. The former accepts all students, while the latter requires an audition to get in.
